The Broker Advantage

Why Use a Broker?

Most of us have been trained, by advertising, to believe that those that offer car and house insurance on TV, in magazines, and on billboards are less expensive due to the middleman (the broker) being cut out.

What many clients don't understand is that they are dealing directly with the insurer who is offering them one product. A broker gives you access to multiple insurers and competitive pricing — all at no extra cost to you.
